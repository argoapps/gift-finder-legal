Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

Gift Finder ("we", "our", "us") cares about your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you use the Gift Finder Shop Mini ("the Mini"), how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

  • Questionnaire Inputs: Information you provide about the gift recipient (relationship, interests, occasion, budget, etc.).
  • Device & Usage Data: Basic diagnostics provided by the Shop platform (e.g., device model, OS version, session logs).
  • No Account Data: We do not collect or store your Shopify account info, personal identifiers, or payment data.

2. How We Use Information

  • Generate personalized gift recommendations via approved AI providers.
  • Improve the Mini's features and performance.
  • Ensure the experience complies with Shopify policies and legal requirements.

3. AI & Third-Party Services

Questionnaire responses are sent to our secure backend, which may use Anthropic Claude or other approved AI models to generate recommendations. These services process the text inputs solely to create gift ideas—they do not receive any identifiable user information.

4. Data Storage & Retention

  • Questionnaire responses are stored temporally to deliver recommendations and may be anonymized for analytics.
  • We delete or anonymize data when it's no longer needed for the stated purposes.
  • We follow industry-standard security practices to protect the data we process.

5. Sharing Information

We do not sell or rent your data. We only share information with:

  • Trusted AI providers who help generate recommendations.
  • Shopify (as required by the platform’s APIs and policies).
  • Legal authorities, if required by law.

6. Your Choices

  • You can choose what information to share in the questionnaire.
  • You can stop using the Mini at any time; uninstalling or leaving the Mini removes it from your device.
  • Contact us if you’d like to request deletion of questionnaire responses associated with your session.

7. Children's Privacy

The Mini is not directed toward children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Updates to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The “Last Updated” date reflects the latest revision. Continued use of the Mini after changes means you accept the updated Policy.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at argowebapps@gmail.com.

By using Gift Finder, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
